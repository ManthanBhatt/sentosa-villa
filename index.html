About Us

The Sentosa Villa is a luxurious villa in Goa, situated few kilometers away from the Dabolim Airport (35kms), with beautiful surroundings, Calm and quite spaces with serene views that would make your getaway an amazing experience. All our villas are elegantly furnished with handmade furniture include luxury en-suite facilities with complimentary amenities. Enjoy the comfort of your home in this villa with all the amenities; with two common swimming pools, party lawn, a play area for the kids, restaurant and bar, personal parking space and personal TVs in all the rooms. You can rent out the whole villa or even individual rooms as you like. All the prime tourist spots and beaches are in the vicinity, in the radius of about 10-15kms. Rent out this villa to enjoy true Goa beauty!
